About Us

Karwaan is a cultural fest, organised by our college students in order to promote art and culture in the college. This includes events and competitions like- Singing, Dancing, Poetry, Nukkad Natak, Drama, Debate, etc. Since the global pandemic is boring us down to our homes, Karwaan will be organised online to smash our roofs and let the rain in. This will be a platform for every artist, to showcase their talent and win exciting prizes. No matter if we are at our homes, we can still feel the mizzle about to come.
Every artist or art lover can be a part of this wonderful mizzle organised by students of UIT, RGPV, Bhopal, irrespective of their age & gender.
